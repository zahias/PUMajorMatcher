PU Major Compass – 15 Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQs)
Each question will include 6 options, corresponding to the undergraduate majors at Phoenicia University.
Once an applicant completes the assessment, the responses will be automatically sent to my working email. Access to the results will also be possible through the IT department, allowing for analysis reports.
The assessment will collect essential applicant information for tracking purposes, including:
Full name
Contact number
Email address
High school
 
This structure will help identify the PU undergraduate major that best matches each student’s interests and strengths.
 
Below is the proposed assessment content:
 
PU Major Compass
Full name
Contact number
Email address
High school
Read each question carefully and choose ONE answer that best describes you.
There are no right or wrong answers.
Your answers will help identify the PU undergraduate major that best matches your interests and strengths.
 
 
1. Which activity do you enjoy the most?
A. Organizing people, plans, or projects
B. Writing, speaking, or creating media content
C. Solving technical or logical problems
D. Designing or visualizing ideas
E. Caring for or supporting people’s health
F. Debating issues or defending opinions
